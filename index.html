WHAT WE DO

BRC and KOSHER Certified “NUTSGE LLC” is a Zugdidi Based Georgian Hazelnut Processing and Exporting Company. As a sister Company to “TREE NUT” and “NUTEX” it was established in 2012, based on the 18 years of Hazelnut Business Experience of the Founders. The company operates 2500 m² production area in compliance with EU standards and produces all range of Hazelnut Products of Premium Quality: Natural Hazelnut Kernels, Roasted Blanched Kernels, Roasted Chopped Hazelnuts, Hazelnut Meal and Paste.
